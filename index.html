Description

The Kawasaki Z900 is a powerful yet user-friendly sportbike, designed for riders seeking thrilling performance with a comfortable ride. Its aggressive styling and strong performance make it a top choice in the naked bike segment.

Engine & Performance

The Z900 features a 948cc liquid-cooled inline-four engine delivering smooth yet thrilling acceleration. It's known for its linear power delivery and responsive throttle, making it suitable for both city rides and highway bursts.

Design & Features

The bike sports a Sugomi-inspired design with sharp lines, LED lighting, and a TFT digital display. It includes riding modes, traction control, and a slipper clutch to enhance the riding experience.

Safety

Equipped with dual disc front brakes with ABS, a strong trellis frame, and modern electronics, the Z900 ensures stable and controlled handling even at high speeds.

Availability

The Kawasaki Z900 is available in major markets globally including the US, Europe, and Asia. Variants include standard, SE, and performance editions with color options like Metallic Spark Black and Candy Lime Green.
